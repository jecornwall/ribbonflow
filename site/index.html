ribbonflow

Animated SVG flow diagrams. Particles stream along variable-width ribbons; the ribbon pinches at bottlenecks, so a diagram shows where work piles up.

See these flows in the talk — The AI Engineer

ribbonflow began as the diagram engine for the talk The AI Engineer, then was extracted into a standalone MIT library.
